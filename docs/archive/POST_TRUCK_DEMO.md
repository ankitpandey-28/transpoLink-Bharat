## 🚀 Usage Flow

### For Truck Owners:
1. **Navigate**: Click "Post Truck" in navbar
2. **Fill Form**: Enter truck details
3. **Select Type**: Choose from 6 truck types
4. **Set Route**: Specify from/to locations
5. **Set Dates**: Choose availability period
6. **Set Price**: Enter price per kilometer
7. **Contact Info**: Provide phone numbers
8. **Submit**: Click "Post Truck Details"
9. **Confirmation**: See success message
10. **Done**: Form resets, ready for next posting

### For Businesses:
- Can browse posted trucks (future feature)
- Contact truck owners directly
- Compare prices and availability
- Book trucks for their cargo
